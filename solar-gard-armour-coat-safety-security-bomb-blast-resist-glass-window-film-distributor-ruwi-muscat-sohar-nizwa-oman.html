Solar Gard Saint-Gobain safety films

Increased protection from bomb and blast hazards

S olarGard Saint-Gobain safety films are the premium quality and highly durable product to resist any kind of risk from the glass breaks due to physical impacts. Solar Gard Saint-Gobain safety films are optically clear and works as a invisible barrier on your glass windows or partitions. The extreme powerful layers of high-tensile polyester and aggressive adhesives are highly resistant to any kind of sharp and flying object which may break and create flying glass shards and causes multiple level of risk to human body. In the event any explosions, untreated windows or glass separations may cause widespread damage by breaking the glasses apart which increase the risk of injury. (in case of bomb blast, the shock wave or flying objects can cause tempered glass break into tiny pieces and un-treated glass can be threat full sharp objects at or more than the speed of sound) Safety films do not allow the glass to break apart and prevent the glass shards from flying in case of any impact.

Solar Gard Saint-Gobain safety window films protect the people in such dangerous situations and keep the internal environment. These films can be applied to toughened, annealed, or laminated glass. They are available in various thicknesses, from 4 ml to 14 ml. The film thickness is selected for level of protection desired and the dimensions of the glass pane. Solar Gard Saint-Gobain safety film can be applied for security applications, which prevent forced entry to your home, office or showroom. (Such as glass doors, home windows and glazing units). Solar Gard Saint-Gobain Safety films have the product range with heat resisting properties and shaded for comfortable view where the sun exposure is excessive.

Glass breaks due to the thermal stress.

As temperature soar in the peak of summer, glazing units are likely to break which may result in the most dangerous risk factor of glass break and flying will end up in loss of life and damage to the properties below. Safety film installed windows hold the broken glasses in single piece and does not allow the glass to fall down unless the glass fixing agents fails to perform.

Protection from the glass breaks during earthquakes

In many parts of the world, natural disaster such as earthquake is un predicted. Ground motion during such disaster cause building movement, which force the glass to break and fall-out, endangering people below or rear most of the injuries due to any natural disaster and other impacts are with the flying broken glass which can be the most dangerous threat to the people. Safety film does not allow the glass to break apart and prevent the glass from flying in case of any impact.

Protects from hurricanes and windborne debris

SolarGard Saint-Gobain Safety films can be used as preventive measure to protect against flying glass when glazing is broken by windborne debris. Safety films prevent windows from shattering, or retaining shattered glazing in its frame if it is professionally attached.

External Safety film installations.

SolarGard Saint-Gobain offers the solutions for the external applications of safety films. External safety films are manufactured with high technology to resist the UV rays and perform for longer period. External films are opted to install where the internal application is impossible due to the non reachability or any other reasons. External safety films with heat resistant properties are installed to prevent the spontaneous glass breaks due to thermal stress.
