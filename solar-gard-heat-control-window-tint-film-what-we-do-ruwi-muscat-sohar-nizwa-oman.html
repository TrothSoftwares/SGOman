What We Do

Solar Safety Film Trading represents the world famous and leading quality product SOLARGARD SAINT-GOBAIN window films as authorized distributor in India. We are specialized in Architectural, safety & Security and Decoration window films application. We are well organized with skilled man power and qualified administration with relevant knowledge in the solar film industry to provide the sun control and energy saving solutions to the clients on any kind of challenging surfaces.

Projects & Installation

Bomb Blast Resistant Safety& Security Films installation

Solar Gard Saint-Gobain bomb blast shatter resistant safety/security films and sun control films installations are carried out by professional installation team of Solar Safety Film Trading in all Oman cities. Professionally installed Solar Gard Saint-Gobain window films are warranty is backed by the factory.

Solar Gard Saint-Gobain Safety and security films are available from the 2 Mil to 14 Mil thicknesses to provide the ultimate security and safety solutions from the event of bomb blast or terrorist ballistic activities. Safety & Security films can minimize the risk of broken glasses shards flying around due to the shock wave of bomb blast or direct mechanical impact on the glass panels. Safety & security films need special skills for proper installation to secure the film with proper attachment. Professional installers of Solar Safety Film Trading can secure your premises with Solar Gard Saint-Gobain products.

Solar Heat & Glare Control Film installation

Solar Safety Film Trdg evaluate and propose professional advise with relevant documental study report of energy saving and help you to evaluate your energy expenses saving and investment return period. Solar Gard Saint-Gobain heat control window films are high technology products and highly durable. SolarGard Saint-Gobain heat control film products can reject the solar heat radiation upto 82% and helps you to reduce to electricity utilization by 30%. Your investment in Solar Gard products can be recovered from the routine electricity expenditure within a short period of 4-5 years after the product installation and helps you to save in the expenses then on.

Decorative Film Installation

Decoration films can be used to customize your facility into a modern appearance. Computer cutting of designs ensure accuracy and perfection in the installation process.
