Solar Gard Saint-Gobain Heat & Glare Control window films

Reject the solar heat and save on your electricity expense.

T emperature in the summer always shoot the mercury near to 50 degree , most of the air conditioning systems fail to meet the cooling requirement to keep the desired temperature inside during this period. As a part of modern architectural concept, exposure to the sun light also unavoidable.

Solar Gard Saint-Gobain Window film Reject the solar heat radiation upto an amazing level of 82% and blocks 99% of the dangerous Ultra Violet (UV)rays while allowing the desired comfortable daylight inside. SolarGard Saint-Gobain sun control films helps to reduce the energy spent in the cooling systems and cut down your utility expenditure up to 30% in the peak summer.

Solar Gard Saint-Gobain heat rejection films are ideal for any property where maintaining sunlight while reducing heat is necessary. Solar Gard Saint-Gobain window films are thin, multi-layered optically clear film that are retrofitted to the inside surface of glass to help you save significantly on energy costs, maintain a more comfortable and productive environment, reduce interior fading and improve exterior appearance. Solar Gard films are sputter-coated with durable, exotic metals such as titanium, stainless steel, copper, gold, silver, aluminum and other alloys. These varying combinations of metals are what gives Solar Gard its exceptional solar performance capabilities and color.

External Application Films

SolarGard Saint-Gobain offers the specially constructed heat control window films for the external applications requirements. External window films are manufactured with high technology to resist the high level of heat, glare and UV rays and perform for longer period. External films are opted to install where the internal applications fails to reach or not adoptable. External sun control films with high heat resistant properties are installed to prevent the spontaneous glass breaks due to thermal stress. SolarGard Saint-Gobain OSW (sentinel outside weatherable) films are durable and warranted upto 5 Years.
