Awareness about Skin Protection Factor (SPF 285+)

Facts about windows and safety concern

  • 90% or more of car windows manufactured today offer minimal UV protection
  • Billions of square feet of architectural glass in buildings including: offices, shopping centers, and homes provide minimal UV protection
  • Root cause to Pre aging and skin darkening due to the excessive exposure to the sun.
  • The American Academy of Dermatology has found a correlation between left-side skin cancer and the amount of time spent driving; researchers found a statistically significant number of left-side skin cancers on areas such as arms, hands, neck and head that are exposed to sunlight/UV radiation while driving

Prevention and Solution
