Dusted and Frosted Interior Glass Decorative Window Films.

F rosted interior decorative window films provide an enhanced look of sandblasted or etched glass at an incomparable low cost. Frosted Decoration film is easy to customize and periodically changed without much workmanship. Frosted and decoration window films allow you to ensure the amount of privacy in commercial or residential spaces. Frosted decoration films provide better improved privacy while transmitting necessary light. Digital printing also an added option on these materials for customized and personalized design such as logos and company captions.

Dusted interior glass window decorative films provide a sparkling sand blast textured finish on the plain glass. Easy installation methods allow completing the large area application with a short span of time period. A customized dusted or frosted interior decoration film application helps to create exciting dynamic finish and effects on your glass partitions or entrance doors. It is a better and economical method of advertising your logo or caption at your reception area or conference hall. Easy removal and re application is added advantage of frosted decorative films for updated in house advertising plans.

Wide range of customized designs and patterns area readily available and new designs matching your logo can be prepared in plotter with accuracy. Our customized special designs in dusted and frosted decoration films includes cultural and historical patterns of Dubai and Abu Dhabi , UAE.

Interior decorative films are durable without any special maintenance. It can be cleaned with normal cleaning agents which are used for glass window cleaning. Decorative films are primly used in :

  • Conference rooms
  • Lobbies
  • Reception area
  • Residential buildings
  • Private offices
  • Glass Partitions

Provides Safety

Frosted and dusted decoration window film is a thick polyester layer which strongly adheres to the glass. It helps to prevent the accidental incidence of glass breakage and glass shattering up to an extent.

Cost Effective

Frosted and dusted decoration is absolutely economical option to replace the expensive conventional sand blast and glass etching on the glass windows or separations.

Improved Privacy

Decorative window films allow light to pass through glass while providing privacy. It is the right and wise decision to install on your rooms where privacy is concerned.
