Frosted and dusted decorative window film is a thick polyester layer which strongly adheres to the glass. It helps to prevent the accidental incidence of glass breakage and glass shattering up to an extent.

Improved Privacy

Decorative window films allow light to pass through glass while providing privacy. It is the right and wise decision to install on your rooms where privacy is concerned.

Cost Effective

Frosted and dusted decorative is absolutely economical option to replace the expensive conventional sand blast and glass etching on the glass windows or separations.
